I Corps Business Cards

How to make your own:

  1. Click here to visit the Avery Design & Print homepage.
  2. Click on the green "Start Designing" button.
  3. In the top right corner of the webpage you are sent to, select the "Open" option.
  4. Download this file.
  5. Click on the gray "Load project from computer" button near the bottom of the popup.
  6. Select "I-Corps-Business-Cards.avery", the file you downloaded in step 4.
  7. Edit the file to include your information, and follow the instructions to print it.
  8. Make sure to print your finished business cards using the product shown below.
